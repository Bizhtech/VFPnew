About VFP

Venture For Pakistan is a platform to help cultivate talent and build Pakistans tech ecosystem from the ground up.

Our initial offering, the Venture for Pakistan Fellowship, recruits top undergraduate students, puts them through rigorous training supplemented with a robust industry-focused curriculum and matches them with hands-on internship opportunities at startups.

Fellows will then receive 1:1 coaching and mentorship from industry professionals at top technology companies such as Google, Facebook, Amazon, Uber etc. This experience consists of direct coaching of how to break into tech, its various career paths and the art of packaging work experiences on to resumes and in interviews.
